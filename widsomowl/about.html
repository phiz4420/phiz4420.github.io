Our Story

Wisdom Owl Books has always been more than just a local-owned independent bookstore. Our mission is to create a community space that makes every reading experience significant. We believe bookstores are not only a space filled with books but a living, breathing, evolving representation of communities and ourselves. Come to shop new, used, rare and out-of-print books and support local business and community.

We began with two simple ideas: book stores should be welcome places for all kinds of readers and good used books should be affordable. Our bookstore offer over 500,000 used and new books in hundreds of subjects.

Wisdom Owl Books buys and sells all kinds of books, and every reader is welcome to browse through our stacks. We think of ourselves as a bookstore for everyone, and our inventory supports this identity. Our bookstores offer interesting choices, good prices, and are a reflection of our open-minded, diverse community. If you live here or are just visiting, give yourself time to explore Smith Family Bookstore. If you live far away and need help finding a book, contact us through Abebooks.com.
